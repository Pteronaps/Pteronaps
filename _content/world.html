About World

Pteronaps World Map:

The main world and map to the world Pteronaps live in!

You are free to include your own Naps into this canon world, though it is not required!

MAIN TARGETS OF INTEREST:

★ Main Building and Dome: The building where the Pteronap Sanctuary workers live and work at.

★ Greenhouses: Where they grow different kinds of fruits and veggies for the Naps.

★ Berry Field: The primary fields where Star Berries are grown!

★ Pteronap Apartments: Where the little Nap residents stay at in the sanctuary.

★ Wing-Clip Mountain: The large mountain South of the sanctuary. Many injured Naps are rescued here.

★ Sepia Creek: The small creek named after Sepia. Many wild Naps get fresh water here, and living beneath the water are Waternaps, the Pteronap subspecies.

★ Evergreen Thicket: The large forest area East of the sanctuary, where wild Pteronaps can be found.

★ Release Zone: Where rehabilitated Naps are released back into the wild. Waternaps are released back into Sepia's Creek or surrounding streams and ponds.

★ Wild Colonies: There are currently four known wild colonies of Pteronaps living around the Sanctuary! It is unknown how many Waternap colonies there are, however.

Meet The Workers!:

Luka | They/Them

Luka is the current head-of-office for the small Sanctuary! With a kind heart and a smart mind, they certainly fit the role to play as the person-in-charge for taking care of the Pteronap residents!

Hana | She/Her

Hana is the worker who takes care of the garden and feeds all of the little Pteronap residents! She also helps with any injuries newcomers may have, as well as giving every Nap baths, hugs and kisses!
