Pteronaps F2U Base

    Click on the image to be sent to the download page for the official, free-to-use base! This base is only for regular Pteronaps, NOT Waternaps!

    The base is a PSD file, and only works on computer art programs. Though, it could possibly work on a mobile art app that uses PSD files! Just make sure to check your device before downloading!

Pteronaps F2U Homemaker

    Click on the image to be sent to the download page for the official, free-to-use Home Maker! You will be able to make your Pteronap their own little home! You may add in your own simple furniture, as well as draw all of your Naps in there!

    The base is a PSD file, and only works on computer art programs. Though, it could possibly work on a mobile art app that uses PSD files! Just make sure to check your device before downloading!
